Delaunay Protein Construct: X3d File Format.

HEAD: Haloalkane Dehalogenase soaked with a high concentration of Bromide.
Abstract:
Haloalkane dehalogenase (DhlA) converts haloalkanes to their corresponding alcohols and halide ions.
The Delaunay construct shows the 310 Alpha carbon atoms (chain A) along with one (out of the three)
bromide ion bound in the active site. The X-ray structure revealed one bromide ion firmly bound in the
active site and two bromide ions weakly bound on the surface of the enzyme.

The visualization was created by using Python and Mayavi Data Visualizer; and the x3d file format was
used to add the visualization to the web page.
